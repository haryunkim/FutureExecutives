Testimonials

"Introduced me to a whole new world"

"When I was in college, I really wanted to work in the music industry, but I wasn’t sure how to get in. After asking around, I was introduced to Monet, the founder of Future Executives, inc. through a friend. At the time, she was working at Penalty Records and they thought she would be a good contact for me. She hired me as her intern and introduced me to a whole new world. After twelve years of being in this business, I can honestly say it was being under Monet’s tutelage that helped me get to where I am today. Not only did she teach me the basics, but she also introduced me to many of the biggest players in the game. Monet taught me executives skills and was always there to guide me. I will always appreciate that about her."

"Appreciates and incorporates the ideas of others"

"It was great to be around someone Future Executives, Inc., Founder, Ms. Wahls while I attended High School. She was such a down to earth teacher, which you don’t always come across these days. I interned for her when I was a sophomore, performed in her production of “The Dance Never Stopped” and she was my coach for our school step team during my junior year. Working with her was a pleasure because she make you feel your opinions matter. She is someone that appreciates and incorporates the ideas of others into what she does. You can always expect to see her modeling and teaching the skills needed to be a Future Executive."

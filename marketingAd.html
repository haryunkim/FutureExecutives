“I was born to do this!” Robin Caldwell is the principal of The J Standard Media Group, LLC, a PR consultancy and media group located in northeast Ohio. Specialties of The J Standard include PR consultancy, strategic publicity plans, Social Media PR and digital interactive strategies, book publicity and packaging, editorial services for publishers and authors, and support servicesfor PR professionals. Robin Caldwell holds a MA in public campaign communication from the Scripps College of Communication of Ohio University.


She is a former adjunct faculty member and lecturer for Ohio University and Cleveland State University. Her PR experiences span over 20 years and she can boast of working for the late Katherine Dunham and Barry White in her humble beginnings in St. Louis, Missouri. Today, her current client roster includes celebrity makeup artist, Tia Dantzler; celebrity hairstylist and entrepreneur Ellin LaVar; and shoe designer and philanthropist Etu Evans.
